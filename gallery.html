  • One of the most well-liked butterfly
    fish used in marine aquariums is the
    black and white butterflyfish, also
    known as the longfin bannerfish,
    Heniochus acuminatus (Linneaus 1758).
    It is sought after because of its
    pleasant disposition, propensity to eat
    prepared dishes, and hardiness in captivity.

  • The bluelashed butterflyfish is
    a species of marine ray-finned fish
    that is a butterflyfish that is located
    in the Indo-Pacific.
    It is also known as the
    eclipse butterflyfish, archer
    butterflyfish, or Bennett's butterflyfish.

  • A type of marine ray-finned fish known
    as a butterflyfish and a member
    of the Chaetodontidae family is the
    saddle butterflyfish (Chaetodon ephippium).
    From Sri Lanka and the Cocos-Keeling
    Islands to the Hawaiian, Marquesan,
    and Tuamotu islands, north to southern Japan,
    south to Rowley Shoals, and in Australia's
    New South Wales, it may be found
    in the Indian and Pacific Oceans.
